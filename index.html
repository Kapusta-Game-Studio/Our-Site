ABOUT OUR STUDIO

We are an experienced game-dev studio developing projects to order, as well as promoting our own projects. We will develop a project for your needs from scratch and bring it to release in app stores: Steam, Play Market, App Store.

At each stage of development, all edits will be agreed with you through a convenient for you communication channel: Telegram, WhatsApp, email and so on.
